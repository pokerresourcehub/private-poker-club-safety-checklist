Private Poker Club Safety Checklist

Traffic and Game Fit Checklist

Short answer: A club is only useful if it has the games you want at the times and stakes you can play responsibly. Bigger is not automatically better.

Questions to ask

  • What times are games most active?
  • Which formats run consistently: NLH, PLO, short deck, tournaments, or cash?
  • Are the stakes appropriate for your bankroll?
  • Does the community tone match what you want?
  • Are union/network details explained clearly?
