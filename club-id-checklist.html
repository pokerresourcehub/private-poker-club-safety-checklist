Private Poker Club Safety Checklist

Club ID Checklist for Private Poker Clubs

Short answer: A club ID helps route you to a specific private club, but it does not prove the club is safe. Confirm the ID, destination, support process, and rules before joining.

Before entering a club ID

  • Copy the ID from a trusted guide or support contact.
  • Confirm whether the code is a club ID, referral ID, or agent/support reference.
  • Check that the displayed club name matches what you expected.
  • Avoid random IDs posted without context.

After finding the club

  • Read the rules and onboarding instructions.
  • Ask who handles support and disputes.
  • Compare available games and stakes to your bankroll.
  • Do not proceed if the process relies on pressure or vague claims.
